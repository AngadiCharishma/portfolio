A Smart Traffic Volume Measurement Based on Deep Learning

The project aims to detect the vehicles and determine the traffic density using videos from surveillance cameras. The AdaBoost algorithm identifies the vehicles by enclosing them in rectangular boxes. After detection, the algorithm estimates the traffic density with 95% accuracy.

Technologies Used: Python,Deep Learning, Image Processing
